About Testa StudyBuddy

Your AI-powered learning companion designed to transform how students study, learn, and excel academically.

What is Testa StudyBuddy?

Testa StudyBuddy is an innovative AI-powered educational platform developed for students at the University of Energy and Natural Resources (UENR). It combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with intuitive design to create a comprehensive study assistant that helps students learn more effectively and efficiently.

Whether you're preparing for exams, researching topics, or seeking to deepen your understanding of complex subjects, Testa StudyBuddy is here to support your academic journey every step of the way.

Key Features

AI-Powered Q&A

Get instant, accurate answers to your questions using advanced AI technology. Our intelligent system understands context and provides comprehensive explanations.

Document Upload

Upload PDF documents and let our AI analyze and extract key information. Create a personalized knowledge base from your study materials.

Analytics Dashboard

Track your learning progress with detailed analytics. Monitor your study habits, topic mastery, and daily activity to optimize your learning.

Smart Recommendations

Receive personalized study recommendations based on your learning patterns and areas that need improvement. Focus on what matters most.

Bookmarks & Notes

Save important questions, answers, and study materials for quick access later. Build your personal knowledge library.

Advanced Search

Search through your uploaded documents and saved content with powerful semantic search capabilities. Find exactly what you need instantly.
